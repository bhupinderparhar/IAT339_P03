Blockhead VR - material design

Robert one of the co-op builders wearing Blockhead VR for the photoshoot. (Picture taken by Bhupinder)

Goal

In the course Material Design at Simon Fraser University, the final project was to create a virtual reality headset using two material, in our case wood and metal. The team consisted of Riley Schina, Robert Hodan, Ernie Tsang and I. We had four weeks to complete the project and the process included ideation, sketching, prototyping, designing and building.
