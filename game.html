Problem & Decision

The team faced many challenges which were: finding four users, a comfortable environment, type of research method, a reliable correlation between the team members and converting the collected data into a report. We decided to focus on friends because they were more willing to participate in the project and would take it seriously. A very big concern we had moving forward was to provide a comfortable environment for all the users and not have any sort of distractions. A key feature of the project was to keep result consistent by having members of the team agreeing on results, which is why a reliable correlation assessment was required. The report portion was mainly done by Kingsley and me while Eloise worked on the summary video.
